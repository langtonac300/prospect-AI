## ⚠️ LIMITATIONS & TRADEOFFS

### What This Does Well
✅ Scales to 1000s of customers with minimal team  
✅ 65-97% gross margins  
✅ 24/7 customer support without hiring  
✅ Continuous optimization (no manual tweaking)  
✅ Enterprise-grade reliability  

### What This Doesn't Do
❌ Custom video training per customer  
❌ White-glove onboarding (it's automated)  
❌ Personalized consulting (use AI instead)  
❌ Dedicated account managers (per-customer basis)  

### Tradeoffs Made
- **Automation over customization:** We optimize for scale, not per-customer tweaks
- **AI over humans:** Agent handles most calls, humans for edge cases
- **Self-service over hand-holding:** Customers get dashboard, not calls
- **Standardized over unique:** All tiers use same platform, just different features
